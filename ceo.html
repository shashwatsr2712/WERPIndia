Chief Executive Officer - WERP-India Private Limited
Job Profile

WERP-India is hiring a young passionate candidate as her Chief Executive Officer. The ideal candidate must be passionate and self-driven for championing the course of Women and Girls Empowerment drive in India and beyond.

Reporting

The Chief Executive Officer (CEO) reports to the Board of Directors and will be responsible for the overall strategic, operational performance of WERP-India as well as the implementation of innovative ideas towards changing the status quo of women in India through social research and volunteering. She/He will champion the future strategic innovations in order to generate revenue and to achieve the financial performance goals set by Management. The CEO is accountable for developing and sustaining valuable relationships with private and public sector stakeholders to ensure WERP-India continuous and sustainable social-business growth.

Qualification

Ph.D in any Women Empowerment related Field. -MBA can also apply.

**Special Consideration for all WERP-India Team (past and current Interns and Volunteers) irrespective of his/her QUALIFICATIONs or years of job experience.**

Experience

5 Years Experience in Operating management of an organization/Business Development/ Marketing/ Research and Academia/ Social-Franchising

Lower Age Limit

18 Years at the time of Application. No Upper Age Limit

Desired Skills and Experience

marketing, sales, social-franchising, business development, finance, Women Advocacy, professional liability, business, research, academia, incubator, innovation, incubation, administration,

Special Qualities

The ideal candidate should be a business industry veteran with a minimum of 5 years of relevant working experience.
